Here’s how to get started.

Loop In Your Family

Invite your better half to join you and have your children check their email for a link to login to their Child Account

Add Some Jobs

Help your child start earning by assigning a task or two in the Jobs area

Reward Your Children

When they finish a job, add a reward to your child’s account. When they make a purchase, create an expense in their account.

Track Expenses

When they want something, have them check their balance - if they get it, expense it!

Rinse and Repeat

Add jobs, reward your children for jobs well done, and let them spend their earnings wisely!
